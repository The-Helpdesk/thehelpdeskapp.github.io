What does it do?
The Helpdesk is an app that allows you to get access to updated, current, and verified technology help, tips, and resources. The solutions in our articles undergo rigorous testing to make sure they work the best and are user intuitive. We stepped into the user's shoes and realized they don't need another login, hard-to-navigate website, or set of complicated instructions. These were the founding principles when we started developing The Helpdesk and we took all of that away when we started building the Helpdesk to provide the best user experience.
Why did we create The Helpdesk?
We started The Helpdesk because we saw a problem in the world: there's no easy way to get technology help. Sure, there are different support sites and apps from companies, but these apps and websites are a second thought that not as much effort goes in too. Most of the time, this leads to a clunky, slow experience that doesn't work for the end-user. There are also lots of community forums to help people; however, these forums get outdated quickly.
FAQs
What's the goal?

Our goal at the Helpdesk is to provide a way to get technology help without phone calls, chat help, & community forums. We assemble a collection of and write the highest quality articles we can. When we see new software updates we update our articles to match the new software, and create an archive for legacy articles.
