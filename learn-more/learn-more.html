Our Values
Privacy
Our team at The Helpdesk understands that everyone wants privacy in our digital lives. No one wants to be tracked or profiled across the internet or be plagued with popups or scam emails. So when we took all of that and created an app where we don't collect a single bit of user data- we don't even use cookies.
Simplicity
To have a good experience with anything, you need it to be simple. Long instructions, complex interfaces, and slow loading times make your experience feel slow and clunky. We don't bog down our site with thousands of scripts and lines of code, but instead, use new technologies that allow us to keep loading times down.


Quality
We believe that every user should have a quality experience no matter what device you're on or what your internet connection is like; your experience should never be changed. Every click of a button should be smooth and snappy, every part of the software functional, and there shouldn't be bugs hindering your experience.
Users First
Whenever we do something, we put the user's experience first.


App Principles
Always up to Date
Once our app launches, we will update it every single week to fix bugs, tweak the interface, and refine your experience.
The Latest & Greatest
We implement the latest web technologies into our app and website to create a smoother, more refined experience.


Customizability
To make the app feel more personal, we will implement color themes, light & dark mode, and other options.
Accessibility
We will be incorporating accessibility features to cater to all users like reduced translucency, bold font, and more to make our app easier to use.
